About Scientific Notation

Scientific notation is a way of writing very large or very small numbers in a compact form. A number is written in scientific notation when a number between 1 and 10 is multiplied by a power of 10.

General format: ±a.b × 10±n or ±a.bE±n

Valid Examples:

1.23e+5
→ 1.23 × 105
-4.56E-7
→ -4.56 × 10-7
3.0
→ Regular decimal number
-0.0021
→ Negative decimal

Invalid Examples:

12..
→ Double decimal point
--234
→ Double negative sign
1.23e
→ Missing exponent
e5
→ Missing coefficient
